Our Story


The Palace Cafe is a nostalgic cafe and wine bar in the heart of the Jaipur. The vision of Seema Sethi and General Manager/Beverage Director Aman Sharma, The Palace Cafe thoughtfully reimagines Rajasthan's rich culinary history through comforting, eclectic American cuisine with deep European influences. The restaurant's design evokes the warmth and casual elegance of the quintessential Jaipur restaurants and bars that serve as our inspiration, updated for today. Our menu spotlights a seasonal selection of salads, vegetables, and Rajasthan-harvested main course. soups, thali, papad and other iconic recipes from a old era, along with a variety of other house-made recipes.

Wine is paramount to the The Palace Cafe experience, with a 550+ bottle-deep list, and upwards of 20 by-the-glass selections gracing the menu at all times. A parade of spirit-forward, expertly-crafted cocktails improve upon the watering holes of days gone by, as do an edited collection of tap and bottled beer.
